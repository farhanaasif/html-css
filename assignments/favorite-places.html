My Favorite Places

I have visited a quite a few countries in the world like Pakistan, Dubai, Germany, Italy, Spain, Sweden, Denmark, Belgium, and USA. All countries are unique in culture, people, food, and environment. I enjoyed visiting all those countries but I love Germany and Pakistan the most because both countries have a lot of historical place and I love visiting historical places. I will share a few places I enjoyed visiting in those countries the most.

Lahore, Pakistan

Pakistan is my native country and my first home. Lahore is one of the biggest cities of Pakistan. Lahore is world famous for it's historical buildings, food, rich culture, colorful events, parks. It's city of love and hope for people in Pakistan.

Many people like Lahore for a variety of reasons. I love it for it's heritage, culture, and food. This city does not sleeps. Shops and food courts remain open at night. People visit here to enjoy best food in the world and see historical heritage.

Favorite Historical Buildings

There are a number of historical monuments there in Lahore and some of them were build in 1630s.

Whenever I go there, I feel the aura of the rich culture this heritage. This heritage includes its way of life, its people, its poets, its artists and its streets. Government is not able to preserve it's historical buildings.

Some of my favorite are:

Royal Fort (1556-1605)
Royal Fort or Shahi Qila is one of the magnificent buildings and landmarks Mughal emperors, covers an area of 20 hectares. Most famous attraction of all times.
Image src: http://food.ndtv.com/
Anarkali Tomb (1615)
This tomb belongs to Nadira Begum, the lover of Prince Salim (the later Emperor Jahangir). She was accused of having an illicit love affair with Prince Salim. Image src: http://www.pinterest.com/
Royal Mosque (1673)
Built by an Emperor, second largest mosque in Pakistan. Interior of the mosque is decorated with white marble floor while exterior is decorated with stone carvings. Image src: mojotravel.wordpress.com/

Food

What one does when he is happy? They go for best food and drinks! People from all over the world come is here for enjoy the World's best food at Food Street in Gawalmandi. Food street is surrounded by centuries-old buildings and places. Some of the famous dishes include Biryani, Haleem, Seekh Kabab, Peshawari Chappal Kababs, and Lahore Fish. It is impossible to find same taste of food any where else.

Frankfurt, Germany

I visited Germany twice in the past two years to meet my family over in Frankfurt. I am so in love with that country because people are friendly and so much to see. I am so attracted the historical building specially churches.

Favorite Historical Buildings

Germany is house of elegant historical churches and forts. Government takes a very good care of those historical treasures.

Some of my favorite ones are:
